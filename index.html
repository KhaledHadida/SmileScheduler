Challenge Statement

With our platform we help dental providers who want to simplify the process of scheduling appointments for patients by reducing time spent making phone calls by using an automated in-app scheduling process providing convenience for patients to freely book based on dentist's availability and Reduce costs by decreasing the amount of receptionists in the office.

Features

Effortless Appointment Booking:

Simplified booking for clinics and patients with automated reminders, notifications, and follow-ups.

Seamless Communication:

Integrated chat system for inquiries and secure file transfers between clinics and patients.

Budget Tracker:

Patients can manage their dental treatment coverage and costs directly within the app.
